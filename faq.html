Frequently Asked Questions

What is D2K ARMY?

D2K ARMY is a Malayalam gaming channel from Kannur, Kerala, launched in September 2019. With over 557K subscribers and 82M+ views, we focus on Free Fire gameplay, live streams, cinematic short films, and pro tips.

What type of content does D2K ARMY create?

We create Free Fire gameplay videos, tutorials, live streams, and cinematic short films in Malayalam, including tips for low-end devices and funny squad challenges.

How can I purchase D2K ARMY merchandise?

Visit our official shop at d2karmy.netlify.app/shop to browse and purchase exclusive gaming gear like t-shirts, caps, and decals.

How can I contact D2K ARMY for business inquiries?

For business inquiries, email us at d2karmy@gmail.com or reach out via our contact page.

How can I join the D2K ARMY community?

Subscribe to our YouTube channel, follow us on Instagram and Facebook, and engage with our content to stay updated.

Does D2K ARMY offer content for low-end devices?

Yes, we provide tips and gameplay videos tailored for low-end devices, such as playing Free Fire on 1GB RAM phones.

Are there any giveaways or events for fans?

Yes, we regularly host giveaways and events. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram for announcements.
