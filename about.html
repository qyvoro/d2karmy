About D2K ARMY

The story behind Kerala’s top Malayalam Free Fire gaming channel.

Join Us on YouTube

Our Story

Founded in September 2019 in Kannur, Kerala, D2K ARMY started as a passion project to bring high-energy Free Fire content to the Malayalam-speaking gaming community. From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a powerhouse with over 557K subscribers and 82M+ views, delivering top-tier gameplay, cinematic short films, live streams, and pro-level tips.

Our name, D2K ARMY, stands for "Dare to Kill," embodying the fearless spirit of our community. We’re not just a channel—we’re a movement inspiring gamers to push their limits and have fun while doing it.

Our Mission

At D2K ARMY, our mission is simple: to entertain, inspire, and unite the Malayalam gaming community through epic Free Fire content. We aim to deliver engaging gameplay, creative short films, and actionable tips that elevate your gaming experience.

Whether you’re a casual player or a competitive pro, we’re here to fuel your passion for gaming and help you #DARETOKILL in style.
